## 🌏 Why Choose a Summer Camp in China? {#why-china}

Spending a summer in China is one of the most transformative experiences a young person can have. For international students aged 9–17, a Chinese summer camp combines:

- **Mandarin language learning** with native-speaking, experienced teachers
- **Real cultural immersion** — not in a classroom, but in the streets, temples, markets, and restaurants of Shanghai and Beijing
- **Global friendships** with fellow campers from across Europe, Asia, and the Americas
- **Independence and confidence** that no textbook can teach

Two weeks of immersive camp can be equivalent to months of traditional classroom Mandarin study. The difference? You're *living* the language — ordering food, navigating metro lines, bargaining at local markets — every single day.

> "I've only been here for 2 weeks and I think I've learned more than I learned in half a year at my actual school. It's a different teaching style to what I'm used to." — **Jason, Full Camp Student**

## 🎯 Camp Programs & Options {#programs}

That's Mandarin offers flexible program formats to suit every family's needs:

### 🏠 Full Camp (Residential)
The complete immersive experience. Includes:
- Daily Mandarin classes (morning)
- Cultural activities & city excursions (afternoon)
- Evening group activities & games
- Shared hotel accommodation
- 3 meals per day
- Airport transfers
- Full-time staff supervision

**Best for:** Students traveling without family, first-time visitors to China, maximum language immersion

### 🌅 Homestay Camp
Live with a local Chinese family for the deepest cultural immersion possible. Experience daily Chinese life firsthand — home-cooked meals, family routines, neighborhood walks — while attending camp during the day.

**Best for:** Teens aged 14–17 seeking full cultural integration

### ☀️ Day Camp / Half-Day Camp
Attend classes and afternoon excursions without residential accommodation. Ideal for families already based in Shanghai or students with existing local arrangements.

**Best for:** Local residents, students with accommodation already arranged

### 🎨 Tailor-Made Camp
Custom-built programs for schools, organizations, or families with specific schedules, group sizes, or curriculum requirements.

**Best for:** School groups, corporate family programs, unique scheduling needs

## 📍 Locations: Shanghai & Beijing {#locations}

### 🌆 Shanghai Camp

Shanghai is China's most international city — and the perfect base for a first trip to China. The camp is **centrally located in downtown Shanghai**, just:
- 🚇 5-minute walk from **Changping Road Station (Line 7)**
- 🚇 15 minutes from **Jing'an Temple Station (Line 2)**

**Highlights:**
- The Bund waterfront & futuristic Pudong skyline
- Yu Garden & the historic Old City
- French Concession streets & cafés
- Day trips to water towns like Zhujiajiao
- World-class food, shopping, and culture

📎 More about Shanghai: [Summer Camp Shanghai](https://www.summercampschina.com/chinese-summer-camp-shanghai-china/)

### 🏯 Beijing Camp

If Shanghai is China's future, Beijing is its soul. Students walk through 3,000+ years of history in a single afternoon.

**Highlights:**
- The Great Wall of China
- The Forbidden City & Tiananmen Square
- Temple of Heaven & traditional hutong alleyways
- More formal, standard Mandarin accent — ideal for language learners

## ❓ FAQ {#faq}

**Who is That's Mandarin, and how is it connected to Chinese Summer Camp?**
That's Mandarin is an established Chinese language school with over 20 years of experience. Chinese Summer Camp is one of its flagship programs, fully operated and delivered by That's Mandarin's teaching team — the same teachers, methods, and philosophy used at their year-round campuses.

**What level of Chinese is required to join?**
No prior Chinese experience is required. Students are grouped by level — from complete beginners to intermediate learners — so everyone progresses at the right pace.

**Is Chinese Summer Camp safe for students?**
Yes. All programs include full-time staff supervision, emergency contacts, health protocols, and close coordination with accommodation providers. The program has run safely for 17+ years.

**Where do students stay during the camp?**
Full Camp students stay in shared hotel accommodation with full-time staff on-site. Homestay students live with carefully vetted local Chinese families.

**What are the benefits of joining Chinese Summer Camp?**
Beyond Mandarin language skills, students gain real independence, global friendships, cultural understanding, and a confidence that shapes them long after the summer ends.

**How do I follow updates and special offers?**
Follow [@chinesesummercamp on Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/chinesesummercamp/) and [Summer Camp China on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/SummerCampChina/) for the latest news, photos, and offers.
